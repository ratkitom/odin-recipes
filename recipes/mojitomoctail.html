Mojito Mocktail

Description

This refreshing minty Mojito Mocktail has all the flavor and none of the booze! Just muddle fresh mint, lime, and sugar (or agave) and top with ice and club soda. Youâ€™ll never even miss the rum.

Ingredients

  • Mint. Cool minty leaves muddled in the bottom.
  • Lime. Bright and citrusy.
  • Sugar. Or your sweetener of choice (agave is nice).
  • Ice. Keep it cold!

Steps

  1. First, muddle the mint and 1 wedge of lime in the bottom of a glass (cocktail glass, highball, something short and sturdy).
  2. Next, add the sugar and more lime. Mash it up!
  3. Finally, fill the glass with ice and top with club soda or seltzer water. Enjoy!
